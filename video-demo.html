About This Video

This demonstration video was recorded using the PartidoGO Figma prototype during our usability evaluation sessions conducted in April 2026. The video covers the primary user flows identified in our evaluation plan, including account login, map exploration, resort lookup, and the booking process.

The recording captures both a facilitator-led walkthrough of all major screens and real-user task completion attempts by four participant respondents.
